initial design prompt: mere ko ek app ka UI designs banana hai jisame hum abhi ke lea 4 roles rakhenge

1 role is customer - jisako tickets raise karne ka option denge ek form rahega or uss ticket ka details bhi dekh sakata hai ki usaka kya progress hai,
2 role is technician - jisako kaam milega tickets ka
3 role is middle man - jisaka basic kaam hai ki wo tickets ko technician ko assign karna
4 role is admin - jisako saari improtant information dikhengi 

ye app ka main role hai ki hum customers ki ambience se related problems ko resolve kar saken 
example.
* Customer ka room ka fan kharab ho gaya hai to wo ticket raise karega taki usape work ho jaega
* Customer ka bathroom ka water tap kharab ho gaya to wo ticket raise karega taki usape work ho jaega

ye ek mobile app rahega jisame customer ki profile information rahengi or login rahega, and middleman koi bhi ticket ko proper kon se department ke technician ki need hai usako assign karega and unassign bhi kar sakata hai,

technician ko jo bhi task milega usake app me wo usape apana kaam karne chala jaega and usaka kaam ho jaane ke baad apana status update karega, or complete hone ka ek proof dega in image or video format ye mandetory hai ticket ko close karne ke lea, or finally ticket ko middleman check karke close puri tarike se karega.
